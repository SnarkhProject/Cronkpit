What's new in Cronkpit version:

Pre-Alpha 1.0:
* Generates a randomly laid out dungeon with several rooms, hallways between them and an exit - the goal is to reach the exit.
* Spawn Zombies and Gold Piles in the dungeon.
* Basic movement + combat system - You can fight and kill the zombies.

Pre-Alpha 1.02:
* Added a new monster, the Gorehound, which can track players by scent.
* Line-of-sight and scent based tracking implemented for monsters.
* Changed dungeon generation algorithm to make dungeons larger + added two hallways between randomly chosen rooms.
* Added random variations to wall and floor tiles.

Pre-Alpha 1.10:
* Gorehound sprite is now transparent.
* Removed Gorehound's sight.
